Tando Allahyar

History

Tando Allahyar is one of the oldest cities of Sindh. It has seen many changes under different rulers. The Soomro dynasty ruled this region about 1,000 years ago. The Soomras1 came in this region after the invasion of Muhammad Bin Qassim in 712 A.D. Tando Allahyar came into being as a small village. Later on, this region was ruled by the Kalhoro dynasty, from 1717 to 1783.
