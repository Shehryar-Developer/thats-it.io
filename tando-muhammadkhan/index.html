Tando Muhammad Khan

History

A famous festival Melo of Hazrat Shah Abdul karim Bulri Waro is being celebrated annually in city of Bulri Shah karim. However, the graveyard of jats located at some 20 km south of Tando Muhammad Khan near the famous Bhuddist stupa is quite prominent. It is also known as Makhdoom jo Kabristan (cemetery of Makhdoom). It can easily be approached from Saidpur village.
