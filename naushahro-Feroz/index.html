Naushahro Feroz

History

The Naushahro Feroze was given the status of District on 15 November 1989 and Before, it was Taluka of Nawab Shah District (Now Shaheed Benazirabad). On given status as district, Naushahro Feroze was placed under administrative control of Sukkur Division however, it has been placed under Shaheed Benazirabad Division during 2011.
