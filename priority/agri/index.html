Agri-Sector Projects

Cluster Facts

SEDF is providing financial support in the form of mark-up subsidy for conversion of agri-sector projects from conventional energy sources to renewable/clean energy. This will reduce the emission of greenhouse gases as well as minimizes the industrial carbon footprint in the Province. Utilization of green energy will give market access into high end European markets.

  • In line with Government of Sindh’s vision to minimize industrial carbon footprint in the Province, SEDF is keen to support Climate Resilient and Smart Agriculture Projects
  • Promotion of Clean / Green Energy will give greater market access to high end European/Global markets
  • Financing for the project will be generated under SBP’s Refinance Scheme for Renewable Energy. Mark-up beyond 2% will be subsidized/absorbed by the SEDF
