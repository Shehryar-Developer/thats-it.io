Jamshoro

History

Ranikot Fort is a historical fort near Sann, Jamshoro District, Sindh, Pakistan. Ranikot Fort is also known as The Great Wall of Sindh and is believed to be the world's largest fort with a circumference of approximately 26 kilometers (16 mi). Since 1993, it has been on the tentative list of UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
