About Bhitai Dental & Medical College
Bhitai Dental & Medical College formed under the umbrella of Bhitai Trust aims to fulfill the growing demand of health care services in the region, provide quality treatment in the disciplines of dentistry / medicine at nearest possible location, as Mirpurkhas acting as a core between diverse cities of Sindh. Bhitai Trust is currently operating a 50 bedded general hospital in Mirpurkhas city at Umerkot Road. Present building of college is built on 2 acres of land having covered area of 25000 sq. feet at Mirpurkhas city in peaceful place and houses the entire basic requirement at college campus. Starting from BDS course, Trust also aims to establish MBBS, post graduate medical education, allied sciences & other educational programs in near future. Recognition & Affiliation: Bhitai Dental and Medical College is recognized by PMDC and is affiliated with Liaquat University of Medical & Health Sciences Jamshoro, Hyderabad. College campus a purpose built state of art building located in a peaceful area having green fields all around, the college is loaded with all modern equipment and training facilities fulfilling all the requirements which are necessary to educate and train the upcoming generation of medical professionals The building of college spreads over 2 acres of land and is purposely built as a dental hospital to host training programs in various fields of dentistry, providing both basic and clinical guidance in one campus.
Bhitai Dental and Medical College believes in Instructional Strategy, learning is best achieved by self-study, directed towards an understanding of information derived from lectures, small group tutorials, discussions, practical work in the laboratories, field work in community and the library. Students are required to apply their knowledge to solve the problems. Bhitai Dental and Medical College & Hospital is fully developed teaching institute, having well qualified and highly skilled teaching staff. The Institute provides best training / teaching facilities like Phantom-Head Laboratories and high class Audio/Visual training aids to impart training to its students according to International standards. The institute has its own fully developed and well equipped Oral and Dental Hospital with all the modern facilities required for training high class dental graduates.
