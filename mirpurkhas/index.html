Mirpurkhas

History

The early history of Mirpur Khas dates back to the 4th century CE Buddhist settlement known as Kao Jo Daro. Much later, armies of Muhammad Bin Qassim settled in the area when Sindh was conquered by Muhammad Bin Qassim, and shortly following that, the city of Mirpur Khas was later founded by Mir Ali Murad Talpur in 1806. The city remained the capital of Talpur Mirs of Mirpurkhas until 1843 when Sindh was annexed to British India under East India Company.
