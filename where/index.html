Overview

SINDH remains aprominnent center of trade and enterprise due to its thriving industrial base, abundant natural resources, well-developed infrastructure, competitive human resources, access to two seaports, sophisticated communication network, modern financial and service sector, and investment-friendly policies. These factors contribute to the promise of Sindh, offering investors excellent opportunitites for growth and prosperity through investment and trade.

The Government of Sindh gives high priority to investment facilitation that paves the way for continuous progress in the province, and helps it achieve its true economic potential. Sindh Enterprise Development Fund (SEDF) is one of the many success stories and initiatives of the Sindh Government to stimulate economic activity in the province.

WHY INVEST IN SINDH?

  • Young and well-educated workforce
  • Strategic location as a regional hub
  • Abdundant and diverse natural resources (coal, wind energy, coastline of 350 kMs, solar etc.)
  • Large-scale reforms and privatization program for an open economy and favorable investment climate
  • Rapidly growing and stable economy
