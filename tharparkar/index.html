Tharparkar

History

At that time, a river used to flow in this region, which was a water source for the Indus Valley Civilization. General Charles Napier was appointed as the first Governor General of Sindh. He made Tharparkar a part of Hyderabad. In 1860, this region was named as “Eastern Sindh Frontier” with its headquarters at UmerKot. In 1882, the British government divided the province into different administrative units.
